What is a bungalow?

A bungalow is a small house or cottage that is either single-story or has a second story built into a sloping roof (usually with dormer windows), and may be surrounded by wide verandas.

The term is derived from the word bangla (meaning Bengali) and used elliptically to mean a house in the Bengal style.

Bungalows are very convenient for the homeowner in that all living areas are on a single-story and there are no stairs between living areas. It is well suited to persons with impaired mobility, such as the elderly or those in wheelchairs.


Other advantages of a bungalaw house:

  • Lighter foundation. It will involve less materials to use.
  • Lower cost of construction and labor. One level will incur lower cost as compared to storey houses.
  • Lower risk. Electrical plans are simpler and certainly, safety risks as well is at lower degree.
  • It will serve the seniors well. Bungalow house will define a perfect living space for the old for ease of movements.
  • It can accommodate mobility limitations.
  • This type is favorable to kid-friendly living.
  • Defined to be an open-plan design. This features a spacious and open with light and air which is enjoyable.
  • Offers privacy. Typically built in nice large spaces, this normally will offer a lot of privacy.
  • Flexible for expansion or additional rooms. Future extensions can be accommodated.
  • Higher demand for re-selling. The availability of great benefits will make it in high-demand.
  • Easier and efficient maintenance cost. The maintenance costs of bungalow type is less than that of other types of houses.
